Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Loopii ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect: account information (your email or phone number, and limited info from Apple or Google if you use those to sign in); profile details (avatar, interest tags, display name); content you create (posts, events, trade listings, and the text, images, and voice messages you send in chats); location data (only if you enable it); usage and device data (device type, app interactions, crash diagnostics, push notification tokens); AI conversation data (your conversations with our AI companion, and content you ask to have translated); and purchase data (records of subscriptions or in-app purchases). If you choose to use identity verification, you also provide a government-issued ID image, a selfie, and a liveness check (processed by our verification provider).

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve the service, personalise content and recommendations, show nearby people and events (with your consent), process AI and translation features, deliver notifications, process purchases, enforce our terms, and communicate with you.

4. AI Companion and Translation

To power our AI companion and to translate messages and posts, the relevant content is sent to and processed by a third-party AI provider, OpenAI, acting on our behalf as a service provider. This content is used only to generate the response or translation within the app. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or sold to anyone. Because our AI provider operates outside Australia, this involves an international transfer of data.

5. Identity Verification (Optional)

Loopii offers an optional identity verification feature; you are not required to use it, though some features may be unavailable unless you do. If you choose to verify, you provide images of a government-issued ID and a selfie, and complete a liveness check, from which facial and biometric data may be generated to confirm the document belongs to you. This may include sensitive or "special category" personal information (including biometric data) under applicable laws.

This data is collected and processed by our third-party identity-verification provider, Didit, which acts as our processor while we remain the data controller. Didit processes and stores it on secure infrastructure, by default within the European Union, under its own privacy policy at https://didit.me/terms/privacy-policy/.

Loopii receives and stores only the outcome of the verification (for example, whether you were verified) and limited metadata; we do not store your ID images or biometric data. We use verification only to confirm identity, deter impersonation and fraud, and support trust and safety, including displaying a verified badge and enabling certain features. We do not use this data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

By starting identity verification you consent to this processing. Verification is optional, and you may choose not to verify. To withdraw consent or request deletion of your verification data, contact support@loopii.io.

6. Data Storage and Third-Party Providers

We rely on trusted providers who process data only to provide their services to us: Supabase (database and storage), OpenAI (AI and translation), Sentry (crash diagnostics), HERE (maps and geocoding), and Apple and Google (sign-in, notifications, and payments). We take reasonable measures to keep your data secure. We also use Didit for optional identity verification.

7. Voice Messages

Voice recordings are captured only when you actively initiate a voice message, and are stored securely to deliver the message to the recipient. We do not use voice data for advertising or profiling. You may delete your voice messages at any time within the app.

8. Payments and Subscriptions

Payments for premium features are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, not by us. We do not store your full payment details; we receive confirmation of your purchase and subscription status.

9. International Data Transfers

Some providers operate outside Australia (for example, our AI provider is in the United States). Where your information is processed overseas, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is handled in line with this policy and applicable law.

10. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the providers above, with other users where you choose to make content public, or when required by law.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time within the app. When you do, we permanently remove your account, posts, and listings, and remove your personal identifiers from shared content such as messages and comments (which may remain in anonymised form so others' conversations stay intact). Deletion is generally completed within 30 days, except where we must retain certain information by law.

12. Your Choices

You can control location sharing and notifications in your device settings. You may use anonymous display names. You may access or delete your data in the app, or by contacting us.

13. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data. No system is completely secure; use the service at your own risk.

14. Children

Loopii is not intended for users under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn we have, we will delete it.

15. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of material changes within the app.

16. Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of Australia, including the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

17. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@loopii.io.
